TS Foods - Online Food Delivery Website

For my second project, I designed and developed "TS Foods," an online food delivery website using a combination of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript.

TS Foods aims to provide a seamless and user-friendly platform for customers to explore a wide range of food options, place orders, and have their favorite meals delivered to their doorstep.

I incorporated responsive design techniques to ensure a consistent and enjoyable experience across various devices. The website features an intuitive user interface, showcasing the available menu items, prices, and special offers. Customers can easily select their desired items and customize their orders as needed.

The use of JavaScript enhanced the interactivity of the website, allowing customers to add items to their cart, review their selections, and proceed to checkout. This project not only improved my coding skills but also gave me valuable insights into creating user-centric web experiences.
